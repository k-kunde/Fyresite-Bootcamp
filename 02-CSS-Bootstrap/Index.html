Drowning Divergent

About The Band

Drowning Divergent is a modern alternative band with a heavy grunge sound, sprinkled with rebellious punk vibes.

Founded in 2021, this new band is headed by lead singer, guitarist and song writer, Kiera Kunde.

While Kiera has risen as the star of Drowning Divergent, the other members still cannot be forgotten. This is because they don't exist. There are no other members of this band. I have no musical friends and no one will start a band with me.
